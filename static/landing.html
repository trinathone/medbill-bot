FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is it really free?
Yes. Every account gets free bill analyses each month — no credit card, no trial that flips to paid. We may add premium features later, but auditing your bill and getting a dispute letter stays free.
How accurate is the AI?
Each bill type runs on a prompt tuned for that industry's common errors, and every flag shows the reasoning and dollar amount so you can verify it. It's a powerful first pass — not legal or medical advice. For complex disputes, confirm with a professional.
What kinds of bills can I analyze?
Medical, legal, utility, contractor, insurance, telecom and property-tax bills today — with more on the way. Pick the type in the app and the audit adjusts to match.
Is my data safe?
Uploads are encrypted in transit and sent to the AI only to analyze your bill. We don't store your bill contents on our servers, and we never sell your data.
What happens after the analysis?
You get a summary of potential overcharges, a line-by-line list of flags with amounts, and a formal dispute letter with placeholders for your details. Copy the letter, fill in your info, and send it to the provider.
